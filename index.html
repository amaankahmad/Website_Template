Welcome!

A little short introduction; I'm Amaan Ahmad, and this is my personal website, regarding who I am, all my projects and aspirations. Currently I am a 1st year Medical Engineering Student at Imperial College London, looking to go pursue Human-Computer Interaction / Prosthetic technologies in the future!

Having started university in 2019, I have become increasingly interested in Robotics and Software development. This interest has taken up the form of Web and App development in my free time as well as more hardware focused projects through Hackathons, my degree projects and the IC Robotics Society. Currently, I am looking to develop my knowledge in neighbouring disciplines whilst making relevant contacts! If you would like to connect - please reach out via the links above or the contact form below!
